Money Management is all about managing your money in the forex in a way that maximizes your profits and minimizes the losses for you. Money management refers to the strategies that you implement in your forex trading endeavors that help you gain profits and minimize losses. It also has a lot to do with money management. Since the forex market is quite volatile, effective money management is essential if you want to stay in the game for a long time.The forex market is not dominated by a single market exchange, but a global network of computers and brokers from around the world. Forex brokers act as market makers as well and may post bid and ask prices for a currency pair that differs from the most competitive bid in the market. The forex market is made up of two levels—the interbank market and the over-the-counter (OTC) market. The interbank market is where large banks trade currencies for purposes such as hedging, balance sheet adjustments, and on behalf of clients. The OTC market, on the other hand, is where individuals trade through online platforms and brokers.
